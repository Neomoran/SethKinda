About Me

My career journey began way back in 2008 at Highway academy where I passed well my KCPE examination. Later on, I attended Musingu boys high school, Kakamega for my secondary education where I passed my K.C.S.E examination very well with maximum grades.Currently, I am final year student pursuing Bachelors degree in Medicine and Surgery at Moi University, Eldoret. I have worked as a trainee doctor at Moi Teaching and Referral Hospital, with successful handling of patients with common known diseases.
